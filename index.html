ℹ About / Help

🩸 App Intro

Free · No ads · No account · No internet · No permissions

A simple, private glucose tracker that works entirely on your device. Your data never leaves your browser unless you choose to export it.

📖 How to Use

  • Real-Time mode: Enter your glucose value and click Add. Date and time are captured automatically from your system.
  • Old Readings mode: Switch to enter past readings with custom date and time.
  • Post-meal checkbox: Check this if the reading was taken 2 hours after eating. It will be tagged accordingly.
  • Export JSON: Download all your readings as a file for backup or to transfer to another device.
  • Import JSON: Load a previously exported file to restore your data.
  • Delete: Remove any individual reading using the Delete button in the table.

💾 Your Data

All readings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Data persists even after closing the browser
  • Clearing browser data will erase readings
  • Use Export JSON regularly to back up your data
  • Use Import JSON to restore from backup
